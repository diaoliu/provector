# Tools

<Callout type="warning">Docs for this theme is under construction.</Callout>

## Selection Tool

- **Select single object**: click an object.
- **Select multiple objects**: drag to create a bounding box around the desired objects.
- **Unselect all objects**: click on blank area.
- **Add object to selection**: <kbd>shift</kbd> Click an unselected object.
- **Remove object from selection**: <kbd>shift</kbd> Click a selected object.
- **Select object within a group**: Double Click a group.
- **Move objects**: drag objects.
- **Move objects horizontally or vertically**: <kbd>shift</kbd> drag objects.
- **Move and duplicate objects**: <kbd>alt/option</kbd> drag objects.
- **Rotate objects**: drag a corner of bounding box.
- **Rotate objects between -45 and 45 degrees**: <kbd>shift</kbd> drag a corner of bounding box.
- **Scale objects**: Drag a side of bounding box.
- **Scale objects whilst maintaining aspect Ratio**: <kbd>shift</kbd> Drag a side of bounding box.
- **Scale objects relative to the object's center point**: <kbd>alt/option</kbd> Drag a side of bounding box.
- **Switch to direct selection tool**: Hold down <kbd>ctrl</kbd>.

## Direct Selection Tool

- **Select single anchor point**: click an anchor point of selected paths.
- **Select multiple anchor points**: drag to create a bounding box around the desired points.
- **Add anchor point to selection**: <kbd>shift</kbd> click an unselected anchor point.
- **Remove anchor point from selection**: <kbd>shift</kbd> click a selected anchor point.
- **Move anchor points**: drag anchor points.
- **Move anchor points between -45 and 45 degrees**: <kbd>shift</kbd> drag anchor points.
- **Move anchor points to a new position**: Enter new values in Panel > Transform.
- **Move handles simultaneously**: Drag a handle.
- **Move handles independently**: <kbd>alt/option</kbd> Drag a handle.
- **Convert to smooth points**: Click Panel > Anchors > Convert to Smooth Points.
- **Convert to corner points**: Click Panel > Anchors > Convert to Corner Points.
- **Change round corners radius**: Drag live corners or enter new value in Panel > Round Corners.
- **Cut paths**: Click Panel > Anchors > Cut Paths.
- **Join paths**: Select two start points or endpoints, then click Panel > Anchors > Join Paths.
- **Remove points**: Click Panel > Anchors > Remove Anchor Points.
- **Switch to selection tool**: Hold down <kbd>ctrl</kbd>.
